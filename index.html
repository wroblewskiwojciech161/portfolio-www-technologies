B.A.S.I.O.R

(Badanie adaptacji systemu i ograniczeń ruchu) - application in client-server architecture simulating the movement ofselected tram lines in Wrocław. It simulates the behavior of how optimally trams should behave during traction failure. It allows you to visualize how the graph of the city's tram networks will change when removing communication nodes. The main technologies are Html, CSS, Python, Flask and the OSMNX library (code available on github)
